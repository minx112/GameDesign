Creators
----------
Bailey Durkin
Grant Sipes
Mitchel Dennon


Synopsis
---------
Infected is a music based 2D platformer where you have to reach the end before time runs out!
Challenging jumping, and an unstoppable wave of enemies will try and stop you.
Try to reach the end as fast as possible!

Controls
---------
WASD to move
E or Left Click to attack
Space to jump
